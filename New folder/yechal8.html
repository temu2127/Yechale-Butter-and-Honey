Our Story

q

Yechal Mulugeta

For over six years, Yechal Mulugeta (40) has been crafting exceptional honey and traditional butter in the fertile highlands of Sekela, Gojjam, in Ethiopia's Amhara region. Drawing on generations of local wisdom, Yechal maintains traditional beehives that produce richly flavored honey from Sekela's diverse wildflowers. His golden butter, churned using time-honored methods, carries the authentic taste of Ethiopian dairy tradition. All products are made completely naturally, without additives, preserving both their health benefits and cultural significance. Yechal sources milk from smallholder farmers in his community, supporting local livelihoods while ensuring quality. What began as a family tradition has grown into a respected business, with Yechal's products gaining recognition in local markets and beyond. The distinct floral notes of his honey and the creamy richness of his butter stand as testaments to Sekela's unique ecosystem and Ethiopia's rich culinary heritage.
